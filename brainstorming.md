## New App Ideas - Evaluate and Categorize

### 1. Travel Planner

- **Description:**  
  A centralized place to organize all trip details, collaborate with others, and access everything offline while on the go.

- **Category:**  
  Travel / Lifestyle

- **Mobile:**  
  - Includes a map view to visualize itinerary items  
  - Tap items to open them in Apple Maps  
  - Designed for quick access while traveling  
  - Offline support for when service is unavailable  

- **Story:**  
  - Solves the pain of scattered trip notes, screenshots, and messages  
  - Helps groups stay aligned  
  - Provides a clean, centralized “source of truth” for all trip details  
  - Optional future AI can help with packing suggestions or itinerary edits  

- **Market:**  
  - Solo travelers, students, families, couples, friend groups  
  - Large audience with universal need for trip organization  
  - Strong pain point addressed  

- **Habit:**  
  - Used heavily during the planning phase  
  - Frequently checked during the trip  
  - Users both create (add itinerary items) and consume (view itinerary)  
  - Not daily year-round, but high usage during relevant times  

- **Scope:**  
  - V1 is simple and achievable: create trip, add items, view itinerary, open in Maps  
  - Map view and offline storage are doable  
  - Stretch features (AI, sharing) can come later

### 2. Shared Recipe Book

- **Description:**  
  A simple way to catalog recipes, share them with others, and optionally create a shopping list based on selected recipes.

- **Category:**  
  Food & Cooking

- **Mobile:**  
  - Users check recipes while cooking  
  - Can take photos of dishes  
  - Easy sharing  
  - Useful for referencing recipes while grocery shopping  

- **Story:**  
  - Makes it easy to share and keep track of recipes  
  - Helps households coordinate meal planning  
  - Shopping list feature adds convenience  

- **Market:**  
  - Home cooks, families, couples, roommates, beginner cooks  
  - Large audience  
  - Real value for shared households  

- **Habit:**  
  - Used during cooking  
  - Used during grocery shopping  
  - Used when adding new recipes  
  - Recurring but not necessarily daily  

- **Scope:**  
  - V1: titles, ingredients, steps, one photo  
  - Sharing and cloud sync optional for later  
  - Clear scope and easy to build
